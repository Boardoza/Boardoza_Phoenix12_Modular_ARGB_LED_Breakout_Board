# Boardoza Phoenix12 – Breakout Board

The **Boardoza Phoenix12** is a compact **ring-shaped addressable RGB LED breakout board** featuring **12 individually controllable RGB LEDs** arranged in a circular layout with an open center. This unique geometry makes it especially suitable for creative lighting applications where uniform, circular illumination is required.

Designed to operate with both **3.3V and 5V power supplies**, the B-Phoenix12-BRK is fully compatible with a wide range of microcontrollers and digital systems. Thanks to its addressable LED architecture, each LED can be independently controlled in terms of color and brightness, enabling smooth animations, dynamic lighting effects, and customized visual outputs. The board is ideal for **photography and videography lighting**, **wearable electronics**, **decorative installations**, and **artistic or interactive projects**.

## Key Features

- **12x Addressable RGB LEDs:** Each LED can be individually controlled for color and brightness, enabling dynamic lighting effects and smooth animations.  
- **Ring-Shaped Open-Center Design:** Circular form factor optimized for camera, phone, and creative lighting applications.  
- **Wide Voltage Compatibility:** Operates with both 3.3 V and 5 V power supplies for flexible system integration.  
- **Modular & Expandable Structure:** Multiple boards can be daisy-chained to create larger and customizable lighting systems.  
- **Flexible Connection Options:** Supports both SMD and through-hole (THR) soldering pads for easy installation.  
- **TTL & CMOS Logic Compatible:** Ensures reliable operation with common microcontrollers and digital circuits.  
- **Creative Application Ready:** Ideal for photography lighting, wearable electronics, decorative projects, and visual art installations.

## Technical Specifications

**Model:** Phoenix12   
**Manufacturer:** Boardoza  
**Functions:** Addressable RGB LED Ring  
**Input Voltage:** 3.3 V / 5 V  
**LED Model:** T3A33BRG-H9C0002X1U1930   
**Number of LEDs:** 12  
**LED Type:** Individually addressable RGB LEDs  
**Logic Compatibility:** TTL & CMOS  
**Data Interface:** Serial Data & Clock (DIN / CIN / DOUT / COUT)  
**Daisy-Chain Support:** Yes  
**Operating Temperature:** -40 °C to +85 °C  
**Board Dimensions:** 39.97 mm × 39.95 mm
